Our shop

We are centrally located in Des Moines, IA. This allows us access to I-35 and I-80 to easily be at most tracks same day. Our founders have been racing wheel to wheel for the past 10 years and have brought home multiple national titles. Through those experiences we decided to go into the business of helping others achieve their racing goals behind the wheel of a Mazda!

Race proven

Our own and customer cars have earned multiple race wins across countless classes with SCCA, NASA, WRL, ChampCar, etc.

Solutions to fit your needs

We can help you with as much or as little as you need to get to your project goals. Just need help tuning your aftermarket ECU? We can do that. Need a turbo kit installed? We can handle it. Can't make it to our shop location? We offer multiple remote tuning options. Our techs have many years of experience on all things RX-7 and NC MX-5

On track support

Need someone to help you at the track with real-time tuning and alignment changes? Our techs can be at your races to guarantee you are competitive for a simple hourly rate to get you to victory lane!
